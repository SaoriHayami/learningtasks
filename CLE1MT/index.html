The Philippines is plagued by extensive corruption, which arose during the Spanish colonial era. According to GAN Integrity's Philippines Corruption Report, which was updated in May 2020, the Philippines is plagued with corruption and criminality in all facets of civic life and across numerous industries. Corruption threats abound in the state's judicial system, police force, public services, land management, and natural resources.
